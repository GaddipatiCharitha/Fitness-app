A fitness app is a mobile application that helps you stay healthy and active. It can track your workouts, count your steps, monitor your diet, and guide you with exercise routines. Whether you want to lose weight, build muscle, or just stay fit, a fitness app makes it easy to stay on track anytime, anywhere.
